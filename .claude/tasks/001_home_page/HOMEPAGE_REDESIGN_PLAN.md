## Brand/Messaging Guidelines

### Voice & Tone
- **Professional but human** - Not corporate speak, direct and honest
- **Confident but not arrogant** - "I build departments" not "I'm the best"
- **Technical but accessible** - Explain without dumbing down
- **Action-oriented** - Focus on building, doing, delivering

### Key Phrases/Concepts
- "Day 1 CTO"
- "Where you have nothing, I build everything"
- "Broad-horizon generalist"
- "Complete technical department"
- "From vision to working prototype"
- "Team assembly & leadership"
- "Knowledge transfer, not dependency"

### What to Emphasize
- ✅ 20+ years experience across domains
- ✅ Corporate experience (BSH, Samsung/Cheil, Ubudu)
- ✅ Complete technical + business understanding
- ✅ Ability to assess talent & build teams
- ✅ Proven portfolio across IoT, robotics, web, DevEx

### What to Avoid
- ❌ Claiming to be "agency" with big team (it's you + team assembly)
- ❌ Overselling capabilities not yet proven
- ❌ Competing on price (position on expertise/value)
- ❌ Generic tech speak without specifics

### Key Insights:
- Not "AI-powered applications" but "Rapid prototyping WITH AI" - AI as tool for speed
- Emphasis on **team assembly** capability - can assess talent, build teams
- **Broad horizons** = understanding complete department, not just coding
- **Day 1 CTO** positioning resonates - couldn't advance in corporate, so building departments for others
- DevStage.io is **product brand** - separate target audience (enterprises with many devs)
- Main agency targets those needing **greenfield innovation leadership**
- Projects linked under each service showing relevant experience
- No pricing numbers but position high through language (CTO, team assembly, corporate experience)

### Tone Clarifications:
- English descriptions (international B2B audience)
- "I/My" not "We" - personal brand with team assembly capability
- Professional, confident, honest - not hiding solo nature but emphasizing complete capabilities
- Focus on what you CAN do and HAVE done, not aspirations

### Service Hierarchy:
1. IoT Engineering (flagship - most corporate experience)
2. Backstage/DevStage.io (specialized product offering)
3. Rapid Prototyping (versatility, speed, modern stack)
4. Innovation Consulting (optional add-on to others)
